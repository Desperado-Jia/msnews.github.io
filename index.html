MSN news

MSN news is a large-scale dataset for news recommendation. Constructed from the user click logs of a commercial news website. MSN news contains xxx users and more than xxx English news articles. Every news contains rich textual content such as title, abstract, vert, subvert and url. And every click logs contains positive impressions, negtive impressions and historical behaviors.
